Server Rules

Roleplay Standards

All players are expected to maintain realistic, serious roleplay at all times. No random deathmatch, powergaming, or metagaming.

Respect & Conduct

Respect staff decisions and fellow players. Toxic behavior, harassment, or exploiting will not be tolerated.

Value of Life

Players must value their character’s life in all situations and act realistically during high-risk scenarios.

Server Lore

Northern San Andreas is a region shaped by political tension, organized crime, and rapid expansion. Law enforcement agencies struggle to maintain order while criminal organizations fight for control. Civilians play a vital role in shaping the economy, culture, and future of the state. Every action influences the evolving story of NSARP.
